Yeild Prep Cook Ready
16 Servings 10 Minutes 30 Minutes 60 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 ounces unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour (sifted)
  • (optional) 1/2 cup chocolate walnuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and chocolate
  3. Remove from heat and stir in sugar
  4. Carefully fold in eggs one at a time
  5. Add vanilla, stir in flour and nuts, and mix well
  6. Spread mixture into greased 8x8x2 pan
  7. bake at 350°F for 30 minutes (avoid overbaking!)
  8. Let cool completely
  9. Cut into 16 squares and serve
